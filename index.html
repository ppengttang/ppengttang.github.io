Short Bio

Peng Tang is now a Research Scientist at Meta. He received his Ph.D. degree and B.E. degree from Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2019 and 2014 respectively, advised by Wenyu Liu and Xinggang Wang. He was working as an intern in the Internet Media group at Microsoft Research Asia, advised by Chunyu Wang and Jingdong Wang. He was working as a visiting student in the CCVL group at the Johns Hopkins University, advised by Prof. Alan Yuille. He was working as an intern in Tencent AI Lab, advised by Lin Ma and Zequn Jie. He was working as an Applied Scientist at Amazon AWS AI Labs and Salesforce Research. His research interests are computer vision and multi-modal LLM.
